Why CentralAI Exists

CentralAI is a Central Michigan University student organization focused on building student capability in AI, ethics, and business applications for career success.

We exist because students are entering a workforce being reshaped by AI, but many still are not learning how to use these tools in practical, responsible, and career-relevant ways.

Our goal is to make that transition easier through education, hands-on learning, and connection with industry professionals.

CentralAI is open to all majors. We care about our members and the future of students at CMU.
